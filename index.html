About Sophia

Sophia is a humanoid robot created by Hanson Robotics, renowned for her lifelike appearance and advanced artificial intelligence. Designed to mimic human expressions and interact naturally, Sophia uses cutting-edge technologies like natural language processing and machine learning to hold conversations and understand emotions. She has been featured in numerous global events, highlighting her capabilities in robotics, AI, and social interaction. With over 60 facial expressions and a skin-like material called "Frubber," Sophia can exhibit a human-like presence, making her an iconic figure in AI innovation. She also holds the unique distinction of being the first robot to be granted citizenship, in Saudi Arabia, symbolizing the increasing integration of AI into society.
